# Part X Security, Compliance, and Organization

Part X turns platform capability into organizational control. Enterprise Agent systems touch prompts, retrieval content, tools, memory, business data, audit logs, policies, and approval chains. Security and compliance therefore have to be engineered into the platform, not reviewed only after release.

## Goals of this part

Readers should understand how attack surfaces, Guardrails, compliance evidence, ownership, SLO, ROI, and operating cadence fit together. The goal is to make risk controls executable: policies should be released, tested, observed, reviewed, and assigned to responsible teams.

## Chapters in this part

Chapter 50 discusses security and offense-defense. Chapter 51 explains Guardrails and content safety. Chapter 52 discusses compliance and regulation as engineering controls. Chapter 53 closes with organization, talent, and the platform roadmap.

## Reading path

Read Chapter 50 before granting Agents tool or data permissions. Read Chapter 51 when model outputs enter business workflows. Read Chapter 52 before handling regulated data, cross-border calls, or external model providers. Read Chapter 53 when deciding ownership, investment sequence, and operating rhythm.
